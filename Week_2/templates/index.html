About Us

Vision

  • To raise world class Computer Science and Engineering professionals excelling in academics, research and providing solutions to human problems.

Mission

  • To develop professionals with strong fundamental concepts, programming and problem-solving skills with exposure to emerging technologies
  • To promote research in the state of art technologies, providing solutions to human problems especially in the area of Health, Water, Energy and Food.
  • To develop leadership qualities with ethical values to serve the society.
